🚗 CAR DESIGNS & INSTRUCTIONS

Build your motor car in one of two ways. Plug Motor A and Motor B into the driver boards as standard.

Option A: Differential Drive (Tank Style)

• Build: Left wheel connected directly to Motor A, right wheel connected to Motor B.
• Steering: Spin motors in opposite directions to spin on a dime, or spin together to drive straight.

Option B: Steering & Traction (Ackermann Style)

• Build: Motor A connects to the front steering axle/rack. Motor B drives the rear traction wheels.
• Steering: Motor A wiggles to set the steering angle. Motor B moves forward/backward.

Mapping & Autopilot

1. Mount the phone watching the floor/arena remotely.
2. Click START FEED, make sure your car is in the frame, and click START MAPPING.
3. The car will wander and map: Green cells are safe to drive, Red cells are obstacles (where it tried to drive but couldn't move!).
4. Toggle AUTOPILOT to let the car navigate autonomously using the live camera grid to avoid obstacles (including hands/feet!).
